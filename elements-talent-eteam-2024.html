eTeam Announces Strategic Partnership with Elements Talent Consultancy

New Jersey, July 30, 2024 — eTeam, a leading global talent solutions provider, announced a new strategic partnership with Elements Talent Consultancy Ltd, a premier provider of embedded talent acquisition, Project RPO and talent data solutions. This collaboration aims to integrate Elements' pioneering embedded solution, advanced recruiting methodologies and data analytics into eTeam's extensive service offerings, while simultaneously expanding Elements' reach and market penetration globally.

"I have been impressed by Dan and the team's exceptional work to date and by integrating Elements' cutting-edge and pioneering embedded recruitment and talent data solutions, we are poised to offer our clients and MSP/RPO partners unparalleled insights and efficiencies," said Ben Thakur, CEO at eTeam.

Daniel Goldstein, Chief Executive at Elements Talent Consultancy, said, "This strategic partnership will provide Elements with the resources, stability and security needed to focus on growth. It will also direct access to 300 of the Fortune 1000 companies, supercharging our growth potential and pipeline." The strategic partnership took effect immediately, with phased integration and service enhancements rolling out over the following months. The terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
