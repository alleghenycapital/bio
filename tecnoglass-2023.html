Tecnoglass Completes $98,900,000 Follow-on Offering

In May 2023, Tecnoglass Inc. (Nasdaq: TGLS) successfully completed a follow-on equity offering raising $98,900,000. Stifel served as a bookrunner on the transaction. Tecnoglass is a leading manufacturer of architectural glass and windows serving the residential and commercial construction markets.
