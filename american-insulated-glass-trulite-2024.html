Trulite Glass & Aluminum Solutions Acquires American Insulated Glass

Atlanta, GA, March 4, 2024 — Trulite Glass & Aluminum Solutions LLC, one of North America's largest architectural glass and aluminum fabricators, announced the finalized acquisition and merger of American Insulated Glass, a respected player in the glass fabrication industry. The addition of AIG broadens Trulite's footprint and allows for expanded capacity to better service the growing Midwest and East Coast markets.

"This acquisition marks an exciting chapter for Trulite," said Kevin Yates, President and CEO of Trulite. "We are eager to leverage the strengths of both Trulite and AIG to enhance our offerings and provide even greater value to our customers."

Vernon Bryant, Managing Partner of AV Capital, noted: "AV Capital expresses gratitude to Stifel for serving as the exclusive financial advisor to AIG." Together, Trulite and AIG will focus on strategic growth initiatives and identifying opportunities to enhance efficiency and market competitiveness. The combined company now has forty-two facilities across North America. Financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
