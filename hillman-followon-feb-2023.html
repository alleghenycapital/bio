Hillman Solutions Corp. Completes Secondary Public Offering

Hillman Solutions Corp. (NASDAQ: HLMN), a portfolio company of CCMP Capital Advisors, LP, recently completed a secondary public offering of shares of the Company's common stock by CCMP. Stifel served as Joint Bookrunner on the offering. Hillman is a leading provider of hardware products and solutions to the home improvement and hardware channels.
