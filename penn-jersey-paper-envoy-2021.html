Transaction Overview

Envoy Solutions Acquires Penn Jersey Paper Co.

Envoy Solutions, the Glenview, Illinois-based holding company, announced the acquisition of Penn Jersey Paper Co., Philadelphia. PJP is a nearly 60-year-old company with a significant presence in the Mid-Atlantic as a distributor of cleaning products, foodservice disposables, and janitorial supplies.

"I couldn't be happier that the Furia family has put their trust in Envoy Solutions," said Mark M. Fisher, CEO of Envoy Solutions. "As we continue to expand our footprint from coast to coast, our customers will increasingly benefit from more products and more solutions."

"Our objective was to align ourselves with a partner who values the strength of the PJP brand and whose culture aligns with our Think Customer First strategy," said Thomas Furia Jr. Envoy Solutions now employs more than 2,200 workers, operates a network of 42 distribution centers and supports more than 45,000 customers nationwide. Financial terms were not disclosed.
