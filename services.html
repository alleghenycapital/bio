Advisory Services for the Lower Middle Market

Allegheny Capital provides transaction advisory and capital markets services to founder-owned businesses, private equity-backed platforms, and corporate development teams across the lower middle market.

01 — Buy-Side M&A
Buy Your Business
Partner with lower middle-market private equity groups and their portfolio company executive teams to systematically grow platforms through strategic add-on acquisitions. We provide end-to-end support throughout the entire acquisition lifecycle, identifying and qualifying potential targets, originating opportunities, and facilitating introductions.
  • Target identification, screening, and outreach
  • Strategic fit analysis and valuation
  • LOI structuring and negotiation
  • Due diligence coordination and deal management
  • Purchase agreement negotiation through close
02 — Sell-Side M&A
Sell Your Business
Guide founder-owned businesses from initial preparation and strategic positioning to final execution and successful transaction. We work closely with business owners approaching retirement or seeking liquidity events to maximize enterprise value, structure optimal deal terms, identify qualified buyers, and navigate complex negotiations.
  • Pre-transaction preparation and positioning
  • Confidential information memorandum preparation
  • Qualified buyer identification and outreach
  • Offer evaluation and negotiation support
  • Due diligence management through close
03 — Reporting
Financial & KPI Reporting
Deliver customized monthly, quarterly, and annual reporting packages that provide real-time visibility into your business performance. We transform raw financial and operational data into strategic intelligence that empowers leadership teams to make informed decisions, identify trends early, optimize resource allocation, and drive sustainable growth.
  • Monthly and quarterly management reporting packages
  • KPI dashboard design and implementation
  • Financial model builds and scenario analysis
  • Board and investor reporting materials
  • Operational performance benchmarking
04 — Technology
Automation
Implement cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions across phone systems, chat platforms, and email to dramatically streamline communications. Our automation services leverage natural language processing, machine learning algorithms, and intelligent workflow automation to reduce manual tasks, freeing your team to focus on high-value activities.
  • AI-powered communication workflows
  • CRM and pipeline automation
  • Document generation and processing
  • Reporting and data aggregation automation
  • Custom workflow design and implementation
